Team Dynamics and Collaboration

As outlined in section ###, our team consists of four members, each from a different country and with diverse professional backgrounds. To overcome potential challenges, prevent conflicts, improve communication, and establish a positive working environment, the team decided to invest in team-building activities before starting the project work.

In our collaboration, team members engage in both group and individual tasks. Group activities and workshops will take place in person, aiming to enhance creativity through direct interaction. On the other hand, individual tasks primarily involve preparation and documentation. Each team member has the freedom to choose their preferred methods for these tasks, as long as they meet the specified deadlines.

For asynchronous communication, the team will utilize WhatsApp.

Tools and Information Management

The group decided to utilize the following tools for efficient project-related information management and communication.

Tool Purpose
Notion Designated as the central hub, Notion serves as a singular source of truth. It houses a kanban board to visualize, coordinate, and document tasks and milestones. Additionally, it aids in organizing resources like papers, interviews, etc., by linking them.
OneDrive Using the university's secure infrastructure, all resources will be stored on AAU's OneDrive, in regards to GDPR regulations. Access to reading or writing will be restricted to group members for confidentiality. Links to specific files/folders will be organized within Notion for ease of navigation.
Figma Employed for graphical work, a collaborative Figma file is set up exclusively accessible to group members. Figma may also be utilized for workshop facilitation, although the team has a preference for analog methods.
WhatsApp Selected for short, asynchronous coordinative communication, a dedicated group chat will be utilized.

While this document was created collaboratively using a shared Word file, the team has yet to reach a consensus on the tool for composing the project report. Considering the necessity for extensive citations and adherence to scientific standards, employing LaTeX could offer notable advantages. However, it's important to note that LaTeX may present a learning curve for a majority of the team. Consequently, the group is planning a brief presentation to provide an understanding of the efforts involved in using LaTeX.

The Google co-founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin encouraged their employees "[...] to spend 20 percent of their time working on what they think will most benefit Google" (reference)

While both definitions emphasize that employees allocate 20% of their time to projects beyond their regular responsibilities, there is a discrepancy in initiative and motivation. This difference may impact the project's outcome. Consequently, the group agreed on ...
